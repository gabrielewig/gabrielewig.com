Photography


I photograph a broad range of subjects but generally focus on nature and landscapes. Some of my work can be found on my portfolio at photography.gabrielewig.com. Many of the albums on the portfolio are not public and require a password to access. This is merely for privacy; ask me personally if you would like to view or dowload any photos.

I photograph on both digital and film media. My main digital camera is the Panasonic G7. It has a compact micro four thirds sensor and great 4k video support. I shoot 35mm film with the Pentax Honeywell Spotomatic and medium format with a Rolleiflex.
